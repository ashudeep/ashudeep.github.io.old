About Me

I am a third year Ph.D. student at the department of Computer Science at Cornell University. I work in the area of Machine Learning with Prof. Thorsten Joachims. I am particularly interested in Machine learning for interactive systems. My research ideas touch the areas of Counterfactual Learning and Inference, and Personalization for interactive machine learning algorithms. I have recently been focusing on Fairness, Interpretability and Transparency aspects of such Machine Learning algorithms.
