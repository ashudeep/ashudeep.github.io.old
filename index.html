About Me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the department of Computer Science at Cornell University. I work in the area of Machine Learning with Prof. Thorsten Joachims. I am particularly interested in Machine learning for interactive systems thinking about problems in the areas of Counterfactual Learning and Inference, and Personalization for interactive machine learning algorithms. Most recently, I have been focusing on Fairness, Interpretability and Transparency aspects of such algorithms.

In the past, I completed my B.Tech.-M.Tech. dual degree in IIT Kanpur (India) where I worked with Prof. Amitabha Mukerjee.
